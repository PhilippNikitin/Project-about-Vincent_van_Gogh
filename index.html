Vincent van Gogh

Here's a time line of Vincent van Gogh's life:

  • 1853 - Born in Groot-Zundert, Netherlands.
  • 1869 - Van Gogh's uncle Cent obtained a position for him at the art dealers Goupil & Cie in The Hague.
  • 1873 - Vincent van Gogh was transferred to Goupil's London branch on Southampton Street.
  • 1876 - Find a work as a supply teacher in a small boarding school in Ramsgate.
  • 1879 - He took up a post as a missionary in the coal-mining district of Borinage in Belgium.
  • 1880 - He decided to become an artist. He moved around, teaching himself to draw and paint and receiving financial support from Theodorus van Gogh, his younger brother.
  • 1886 - Van Gogh moved to Paris and get acquainted with many famous artists including Pissarro, Degas, Toulouse-Lautrec.
  • 1888 - He moved to southern France, where he painted many of his famous works like "Orchard in Blossom near Arles", "Starry night".
  • 1890 - Dies at the age of 37.
